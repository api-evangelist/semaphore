Developers are no longer spending most of their time inside traditional IDEs. Today, workflows increasingly happen inside AI-powered coding environments like Claude Code, Cursor, and Codex. Developers are collaborating with agents, asking questions in natural language, debugging through conversations, and automating repetitive work directly from their coding environment.

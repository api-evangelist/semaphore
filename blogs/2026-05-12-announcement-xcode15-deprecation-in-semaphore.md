MacOS Xcode15 reached the end of its supported lifecycle on Semaphore. To ensure the security, reliability, and maintainability of our platform, we are announcing the deprecation and end of life of MacOS Xcode15 build environments. Semaphore users should consider switching to Xcode26 as Xcode16 is scheduled for deprecation in September 2026.
